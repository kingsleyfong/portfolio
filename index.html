Live Temperature Sensor

IDE platform design

The project I enjoyed working on the most using IDE platforms such as ESP8266 and Arduino MEGA, is a cooling system which involves the use of the DHT-11 and HC-sr04, and TCRT5000 infrared sensors to actively read for heat signatures within a radius as it rotates on a fixed platform. If the temperature recorded is above that designated in the program & code, a coolant system (humidifier and fan) is turned on to lower the temperature in the detected area. ​
